Who we are?

CodeChef SCOE is a non-profital technical student's chapter under the alliance of CodeChef, a product of Unacademy. We are a team of Enthusiastic coders who aim to promote Competitive Coding in our Campus. We organise events related to Competitive Programming and help students to understand Data Structures and Algorithms required to solve the problems.
